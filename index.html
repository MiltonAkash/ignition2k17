Mr. Mechanical

IGNITION is proud to announce the introduction of the most prestigious and proud event – “ MR MECHANICAL

Mr Mechanical is an event that seeks the best of the best mechanical undergraduate. The event will test the overall skill based on the following events:
  1. Nuts and bits - 15 marks
  2. Assembly - 15 marks
  3. Machining masters - 30 marks
  4. Personal interview - 40 marks

ELIGIBILITY

Every single mechanical lover is welcomed to participate. There will be a screening preliminary quiz round to short list about 20 participants to actively participate in the events of Mr. Mechanical. The events will have an elimination depending upon performances of candidates in each round and the candidate to secure the most marks out of 100 will be crowned MR MECHANICAL .

CANDIDATES TAKING PART IN MR MECHAINCAL ARE REQUESTED TO NOT TAKE PART IN ANY OTHER EVENT AS THERE CAN BE A CLASH OF TIMINGS AND IF THE CANDIDATE DOESNOT ARRIVE ON TIME FOR THEIR RESPECTIVE EVENTS DISQUALIFICATION FROM MR MECHNACIAL CAN TAKE PLACE.

PRIZES:


Winner MR MECHANICAL – 3000 INR
Runner up MR MECHANICAL – 1500 INR

NUTS AND BITS:

This is an event in which the participants will have to identify the displayed mechanical instruments and name them correctly. There will be an elimination for the least performers and the best performers will find themselves at the top of the leader board.

ASSEMBLY:

This event will truly test the skills that a mechanical engineer must possess in the art of assembling machines. Participants will be judged on their speed and overall stability of the component assembled.

MACHINE MASTERS:

Precision achieved by automated machines is nothing special, but the perfection that the inverter of such machines is what we are hunting for in this event. A basic operation on a work piece using a lathe is all u have to do, sounds easy right but remember one wrong feed and guess what it’s over. The criteria of judgment will be on the precision and accuracy dimension, surface finish and time taken to machine the given work piece.

PERSONAL INTERVIEW:

This is the last hurdle and if u have reached this far it’s time to put your game face on as this is the last event. A qualified professor of the Department of Mechanical Engineering will have a one to one interview with each candidate and depending on their performances the judgment will be given. This is the most important event and has a maximum of 40 point up for grabs.

The combination of all marks secured will be tallied and candidate who has secure the highest points will be crowned MR MECHANICAL

About us

Ignition is a national level technical symposium hosted by the department of mechanical engineering of Sri Venkateswara College of engineering. Ignition hosts technical and non-technical events for young aspiring mechanical engineering students to actively take part and test their skills on both practical and theoretical knowledge on mechanical engineering . Ignition2k17 this time celebrates its 26 year and we the members of ignition2k17 hope to see you at this national level symposium.
